We are a non-profit organization that Teaches Gurmukhi and Gurbani

Our Mission

‘Aap Saware Main Miley’ which translates to ‘Self Introspection Only Leads To Introduction To Self.’

The Nanak philosophy teaches that when we peep inside ourselves, introspect and improve ourselves, the world around us changes. The society that we live in today is the result of our thought processes, circumstances, knowledge, and more.

In this new world, we are responsible for the growth and harmony of the world around us. This won’t be possible if we are not harmonious from within.

Together, let’s pledge to leave the world better than we were born into.

Our Vision

Emergence of a New Society

Human race is the supreme creation of nature and the most intelligent species of all. Yet, we fall prey to our own methods and our self-created drenches as we go against the laws of nature. Ultimately, it hinders the elevation of the human race.

Everyone wants a society that is free from these vices and evils. A society that is free from the untold dread of forced social dogmas that endorse double standards. One that doesn’t indulge us in myths and fears and doesn’t make us run away from the actuality of our lives and our purpose.

Jeevan Jaach is here to wake all of us up from the deep slumber that society has put us in. This is our modest step of establishing the building blocks of this new society.

Jeevan Jaach is an Online Institute for Learning Gurmukhi and Gurbani
