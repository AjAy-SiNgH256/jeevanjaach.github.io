Our Story

Our story started with a modest summer camp in a Gurudwara in Mumbai, where we taught kids the Punjabi language. Summer after summer, we used to conduct the classes for specific age groups, hoping they learned a lot and moving on with our lives for the rest of the year.

But then came 2020. Life in 2020 was different under the lockdown after the pandemic hit the world. In circumstances where it was easy for us to crumble, we were reborn like the Pheonix. We bid adieu to the physical classes and stepped into the digital sphere. We matched our steps with the world and went digital with our Punjabi classes. The first-of-its-kind online FREE Punjabi class that where we taught Gurmukhi and Gurbani went live on 28th April, 2021.

Jeevan Jaach literally translates to 'Way Of Life'.

FAQ

Are these live classes or video tapped recordings will be shared?

We have a team of experienced teachers who have volunteered to take the live classes everyday.

I am not from India, can I attend these classes?

We have been accommodating learners from many parts of the world like USA, UK, Australia, European & Asian Countries. The timings provided will be suitable for most countries.

Do I need to buy any study material?

All study resources will be provided by us digitally.

Will I get the timings I select?

The timings will be decided as per the teams’ discretion. Choose from the options provided in the form. (we will try our best to provide you with the timing you have selected)

Do you offer Sangeet or Instruments classes?

We have taken the initiative to introduce Gurmat sangeet classes in order to support Raag Vidya (Only Sangeet classes are chargeable. We only act as a mediator to connect the students to Ustadji)

Who can enroll?

Anyone who wants to learn Gurmukhi & Gurbani regardless of Age, Gender, Religion, Qualifications and location. We have learners from 3 to 75 years old who have successfully completed the course with us.
